# Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation Day 1

**Date:** Sunday, September 20, 2009

**Title:** Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation Day 1

**Objectives:** The children will be able to

- identify that God created light on the first day
- identify that God separated the light from the darkness
- identify that God called the light day and the darkness night

**Review:** Prayer and questions from last week’s lesson.

**Vocabulary Words:**  
[Creation Day 1 Word Wall](https://1drv.ms/b/s!Ar6LKlEjjtdHz3Ps7LOiggUueboW) 

**Plan:**

### Circle Time:

Read story: “Creation Day 1” from your own story book. Then ask questions from below.

- What did God create on the first day? (Light)  
- What did God do with the light? (Separated it from the darkness)  
- What did God call the light? (Day)  
- What did God call the darkness? (Night)
