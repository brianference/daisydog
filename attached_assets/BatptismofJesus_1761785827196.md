### Circle Time:

Read story: ‚ÄúThe Baptism of Jesus‚Äù from your own story book. Then ask questions from below.

- Who baptized Jesus? (John the Baptist)  
- Where did Jesus go to be baptized? (The Jordan River)  
- What did John the Baptist wear? (Camel hair)  
- What did John the Baptist eat? (Locusts and honey)  
- What did John the Baptist call people? (Brood of vipers)  
- What did John the Baptist say people must do to show they have changed their hearts? (Share with the poor, be honest, etc.)  
- Why did John the Baptist think he should be baptized by Jesus? (He said that Jesus was greater than him.)  
- What happened when Jesus was baptized? (The heavens opened, the Spirit of God descended like a dove, and a voice from heaven said, ‚ÄúThis is my beloved Son, with whom I am well pleased.‚Äù)  
- What did God call Jesus? (My beloved Son)  
- What did God say He was well pleased with? (Jesus)  

**Discussion Questions:**  

- Why do you think Jesus was baptized? (To show us how to live, to fulfill all righteousness, etc.)  
- What does baptism mean? (We are washed clean from sin, we become children of God, etc.)  
- Why do we get baptized? (To be saved, to become part of God‚Äôs family, etc.)
