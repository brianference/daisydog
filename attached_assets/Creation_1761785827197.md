# Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation

**Date:** Sunday, September 13, 2009

**Title:** Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation

**Objectives:** The children will be able to

- identify that God created the world in 6 days
- identify what God created on each day
- identify that God rested on the 7th day
- discuss why God created the world

**Review:** Prayer and questions from last week’s lesson.

**Plan:**

### Circle Time:

Read story: “Creation” from your own story book. Then ask questions from below.

- What did God create on the first day? (Light)  
- What did God create on the second day? (Sky)  
- What did God create on the third day? (Land, seas, plants)  
- What did God create on the fourth day? (Sun, moon, stars)  
- What did God create on the fifth day? (Birds, fish)  
- What did God create on the sixth day? (Animals, people)  
- What did God do on the seventh day? (He rested)  
- Why did God create the world? (To show his love, for us to enjoy, etc.)  

**Discussion Questions:**  

- What is your favorite part of creation? Why?  
- How can we take care of God's creation?  

### Songs and/or Finger Plays:

Introduce songs and/or finger plays: What did God create on the first day?

**Creation Finger Play:**  
(Do actions as you say the poem.)  
On the first day God made light (turn on light)  
And called it day and night. (day/night motion)  
On the second day God made the sky (arms up)  
And waters below. (arms down)  
On the third day God made land (stomp feet)  
And plants to grow. (wiggle fingers)  
On the fourth day God made sun (circle arms overhead)  
Moon and stars so bright. (twinkle fingers)  
On the fifth day God made birds (flap arms)  
And fish to swim. (swim motion)  
On the sixth day God made animals (roar)  
And man in his image. (point to self)  
On the seventh day God rested (lie down)  
From all his work. (sleep)
