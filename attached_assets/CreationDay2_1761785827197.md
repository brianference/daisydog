# Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation Day 2

**Date:** Sunday, September 27, 2009

**Title:** Lesson Plan- (Pre K - K): Creation Day 2

**Objectives:** The children will be able to

- identify that God created the sky on the second day
- identify that God separated the waters above from the waters below
- identify that God called the waters below seas and the dry ground land

**Review:** Prayer and questions from last week’s lesson.

**Vocabulary Words:**  
[Creation Day 2 Word Wall](https://1drv.ms/b/s!Ar6LKlEjjtdHz3Ps7LOiggUueboW) 

**Plan:**

### Circle Time:

Read story: “Creation Day 2” from your own story book. Then ask questions from below.

- What did God create on the second day? (Sky)  
- What did God do with the waters? (Separated the waters above from the waters below)  
- What did God call the firmament? (Sky)  
- What did God call the waters below? (Seas)  
- What did God call the dry ground? (Land)
